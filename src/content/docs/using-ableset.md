---
title: Using Ableset
---

With Ableset installed and running :

## Ethernet connection
1. [Configure the IP address on the controller and the computer.](connecting-the-device.md#ethernet-connection)
2. **ABSLT 1** should turn **green** on the top bar of the controller
3. Press and hold the Encoder and select Ableset

## USB connection
1. Launch the Show Control application, which will convert Ableset OSC messages into MIDI messages.
2. **ABLST 1** will turn **white**.


## Features
### Display

The display shows (top to bottom):

- **Active Song name**
- **Active Section name**
- **Song progress bar**
- **Song remaining time** and **Songs counter**
- **Additional information** (Ableset's [Locator Description](https://ableset.com/docs/locator-notation#locator-descriptions))
- **Next Song name**

### Buttons

The functions of the **5 Buttons** are:

|        | Button 1  | Button 2  | Button 3  | Button 4  | Button 5  |
|--------|-----------|-----------|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| **Shift Off** |Play from Song start|  Stop     |  Previous Song| Next Song| Toggle Loop|
| **Shift On**  | Resume playback | Stop and go to next Song|Previous Section|Next Section|           |
| **Hold**  |  | | Skip Backward|Skip Forward|           |

### Encoder

The functions of the **Encoder** are:

|        | Encoder  | 
|--------|-----------|
| **Single Press** |Song Menu| 
| **Shift Press**  |Sections Menu |
| **Double Press**  |Setlist Menu|

### Pedals

|        | Pedal 1|Pedal 2| 
|--------|-----------|-----------|
| **Single Tap** |Play/Stop Toggle|Loop Toggle| 
| **Double Tap**  |Next Song |Previous Song |

## Redundancy
In case of redundant setup, the controller will show **ABLST 2** in green.

The redundancy itselft is handled by Ableset.
